Hyeon Been Lee

I am a Ph.D. student at Kyungpook National University (South Korea) under the supervision of Prof. Jong Soo Park (Microbial Oceanography Lab). From January to February 2026, I joined the Roger Lab as a visiting Ph.D. student, where I received training in genomics and transcriptomics.

My research focuses on halophilic protists, particularly members of the Heterolobosea (Discoba), with an emphasis on their biodiversity, mechanisms of salinity adaptation, and evolutionary history. I have reported two novel halophilic heterolobosean from Korean solar salterns and demonstrated intracellular ion accumulation patterns using ion imaging. In addition, I have conducted comparative studies of metabarcoding primer sets for assessing bacterial diversity in marine environments.
