Postdoctoral Scholar

Hi. I have been a JSPS postdoc in Roger’s lab since May 2024.

Interests

My interests are phylogenetic analysis and methodology. I am focusing on mixture models for phylogenetic inferences, aiming to develop more flexible models to resolve deep evolutionary relationships among eukaryotes and/or microbes.

Background

I did my undergrad to PhD studies in Yuji Inagaki’s lab at University of Tsukuba, Japan. I learned bioinformatics and basic biological experimental techniques during my studies on DNA polymerases. In parallel with my PhD, I also conducted research on genome annotation using deep learning in a computer science lab, which led to an additional Master’s degree
